Caaaaaarispy Pork Belly

Looks good, don't it!

Errybody like pork, cook it well and get that skin caaaarispy. I wanna hear that cruch from New Zealand.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Belly
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Get that oven hot.
  2. Score the skin side, add salt and pepper. Oil that pork.
  3. Put in over tray, skin side down. Put a second tray on top to hold it down.
  4. Grill for 10 minutes.
  5. Take the second tray off and flip to skin side up.
  6. Grill till skin golden brown and caaaarispy.
  7. Get that crunch.
  8. Never look for another recipe.
